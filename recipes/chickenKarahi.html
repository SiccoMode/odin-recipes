Chicken Karahi

  • 400g Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 can (400g) San Marzano tomatoes, peeled
  • 1 Green bell pepper, or Poblano pepper, or Jalapeno
  • Few cloves of garlic
  • Nub of ginger
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garam Masala
  1. Heat a wok over high heat and add a shot of olive oil to the pan. Cut the chicken into bite-sized bits and add it to the pan
  2. Dice the pepper, smash (and optionally dice) the garlic, rasp the ginger and add to the pan once the chicken has started to brown.
  3. When the vegetables and aromatics start to soften, add the canned tomatoes
  4. Add the garam masala, salt and pepper. Bring it to medium-low heat to let the sauce thicken for ~5 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
